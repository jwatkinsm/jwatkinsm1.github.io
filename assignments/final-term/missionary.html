Full-Time Mission Stays

As a missionary, this means you are temporarily living near a temple for year(s). We provide affordable kitchenette suites that include everything you need to be comfortable and able to stay focused on your service.

Kitchen

The kitchen is spacious and provides the following:

  • Full Size Refrigerator
  • Stove and Microwave
  • Crock Pot
  • Oven and cooking sheets
  • Pots and Pans
  • Dishes and Utensils for 8
  • Cleaning supplies and Towels

Restocking and towel cleaning provided weekly or as needed. Complete maid service is available for an extra fee.

Dining Room

This suite is setup to entertain up to 8 people. You will find ample room to have visitors and feed them.

Master Bedroom

The master bedroom provides the following:

  • King Size Bed
  • Internet Television and DVD Player
  • Connected Bathroom with Jetted Tub
  • Separate room for bathroom
  • Bench for sitting and changing
  • Accessibility Options
  • Fresh Towels weekly

Living Room

We know it won't quite be like home, but it will come close.

  • Open area for relaxing and/or entertaining guests
  • Sofa Bed
  • Gas Fireplace
  • Window Seats for Studying or Casual Reading
  • Large Television and DVD Player
  • Desk with Power and Ethernet
  • Sofa Chairs
